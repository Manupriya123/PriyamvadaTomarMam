About

Dr. Priyamvada Tomar is a gynaecologist and infertility specialist. Dr. Tomar has been providing medical facilities through her practice in Shamli (Uttar Pradesh) for more than two decades. She the been the Director of Genesis Women’s Clinic and IVF Centre, Noida (Uttar Pradesh). Dr. Tomar was born in a family where patriotism was an integral part of her upbringing as she saw her elders determinedly work towards the betterment of the nation. Her family members gave paramount value to working for others and therefore as a consequence of always being amidst dedicated nationalists she also aspired to work for bettering the society. With the goal of working for the progress of the disadvantaged sections of the society Dr. Tomar formed the Purna Prakash Trust.

Healthcare
Healthcare

With a rich history spanning 30 years as a women's health specialist, I have been dedicated to advancing medical care. As the operator of a cutting-edge Test Tube Baby Center in Noida, I bring modern facilities to the forefront. Additionally, my commitment extends to rural healthcare, where I have organized and conducted over 500 complimentary medical camps, addressing the critical needs of underserved communities.

Social Work
Social Work

Founder of the Prakash Trust, an organization involved in various social welfare projects. General Secretary, Uttar Pradesh State: National Security Awareness Forum.

Political Work
Political Work

As the Regional VP of Women's Wing (2014-15), I played a key role in the 2014 Kairana Lok Sabha elections. I then served as State Coordinator for "Save the Daughter, Educate the Daughter" (2015-17). In 2017, I contributed to the Election Management Committee. In the 2018 Kairana Lok Sabha by-elections, I was Women's Lok Sabha Coordinator. From Aug 2017 to Apr 2021, I served on the UP State Women's Commission, showcasing unwavering commitment.

My Family

I am from a staunchly patriotic and nationalist family in Western Uttar Pradesh. Since the establishment of the Jan Sangh, my family has been dedicated to the party and has remained committed to it throughout..

My Grandfather

My grandfather, Shri Purnachand Arya Sanyas, later known as Purnanand Saraswati, was a renowned Arya scholar and freedom fighter. He actively participated in the non-cooperation movement, Salt Satyagraha, and protested against Hindu atrocities in Hyderabad. In 1948, due to his association with the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh, he was arrested. He played a key role in the Shuddhi movement, initiating hundreds into Hinduism, and led movements against untouchability, cow protection, and for the promotion of Hindi.

My Father

Shri Ved Prakash earned fame through his honesty and efficiency by holding various posts as a Gazetted Officer in the Consolidation Department, Uttar Pradesh Government. He strengthened the roots of BJP by contesting as a BJP candidate from Baghpat Legislative Assembly in 1993 and 1996 and becoming the runner-up in this prestigious constituency.

My Uncle

Late Dr. Shivraj had unsuccessfully contested the elections in 1967 as a Bharatiya Jana Sangh candidate.

My Younger Brother

My younger brother Vijayendra, Indian Administrative Service (IAS 1991 batch) Additional Chief Secretary, Assam, has retired in March 2023. The rest of the family is also educated and imbued with nationalist ideas.
